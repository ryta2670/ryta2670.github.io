The first project is an exploration into what could have made an already existing website better. For this website, BeHeardBoulder, we conducted interviews and observations in order to receieve feedback on the usability and aethetic of the website. Most of our feedback can be summarized by this infographic we created in photoshop. In the end, the city of Boulder asked us to send them a picture of our infographic, so that they might use our reccomendations to help in the development of their website and goal of getting voices of Boulder heard.
